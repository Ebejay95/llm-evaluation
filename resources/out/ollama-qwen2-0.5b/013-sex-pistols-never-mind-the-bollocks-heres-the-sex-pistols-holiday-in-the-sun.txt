"Never Mind The Bollocks" is a song by the Sex Pistols from their 1975 album "The Piper At The Gates Of Winter". It was released as the lead single from the album. The lyrics are about the band's dissatisfaction with the government and its policies, particularly the idea of people being forced to work in factories during the winter months. The song is a protest song that expresses anger towards the government for not doing enough to protect workers during harsher times like the winter months.
